hi again! I'm Annie.

My work delves into the micro-world of cell biology, reaches to large philosophical questions of our own cognition and consciousness, and circles back around to design as a way to communicate and create sustainable change in my community and beyond.

I am currently a third year in the Brown|RISD Dual Degree Program, studying Biology and Behavioral Decision Sciences at Brown, & Industrial Design at RISD. I am interested in the intersection of biology, technology, and design, especially biomimicry/biodesign, human factors, and sustainable design. I pride myself in bringing thoughtful creativity, openness, and a balance between detail-oriented and big-picture thinking to everything I pursue.
