M E T A L   I

aluminum & tin material studies • form exploration

✧

For my Aluminum final, I designed a nested triangular pyramid form with a removable exterior. The inner pyramid is kept in place through a tension fit with wire rings, and the outer pyramid is held in place through a tension fit with steel pins. The edges of each triangular piece was hand-chamfered to a specific angle through the creation and use of tin-plated wood jigs.
